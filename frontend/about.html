What is DietGenie?

DietGenie is an AI-powered diet planner that creates personalized meal plans tailored to your age, gender, and health conditions. Unlike generic diet apps, we focus on Indian foods and locally available ingredients for realistic, sustainable nutrition.

Our intelligent system understands the nutritional needs of diverse populations and creates meal plans that respect cultural preferences while optimizing for health outcomes.

Whether you're managing diabetes, working on weight loss, or simply seeking a balanced lifestyle, DietGenie provides science-backed recommendations that fit seamlessly into your daily routine.
